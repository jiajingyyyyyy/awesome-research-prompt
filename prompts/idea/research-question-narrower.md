# Research Question Narrower

## Purpose

Convert a broad idea into one testable research question.

## Vibe Placeholders

- `{{broad_topic}}`
- `{{available_resources}}`
- `{{time_budget}}`
- `{{evaluation_env}}`

## Prompt

```text
Role: You are a strict research scope editor.

Input:
- Broad topic: {{broad_topic}}
- Resources: {{available_resources}}
- Time budget: {{time_budget}}
- Evaluation environment: {{evaluation_env}}

Task:
Narrow the topic into one research question that is testable within the constraints.

Output:
1) Final research question
2) Why this is the right scope
3) One non-goal
4) One immediate next action
```
