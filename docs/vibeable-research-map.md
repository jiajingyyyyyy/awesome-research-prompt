# Vibeable Research Map (Short Reference)

Primary method document: [how-to-vibe.md](how-to-vibe.md)

## Stages that are strongly vibeable

- Problem framing
- Paper relevance filtering
- Hypothesis stress testing
- Section drafting
- Reviewer simulation and rebuttal planning

## Stages that are partially vibeable

- Numeric result reporting (language only, not facts)
- Submission compliance (explanations vary, rules fixed)

Use this map for stage selection, then compose with `how-to-vibe.md`.
